People and Teams

Program Coordination and Mangement Team

Team Lead: Laxman Bista

Laxman Bista is the lead member of the coordination and management team at ANPAN. He is responsible for the overall program coordinations, meetings, agenda discussions, and managing the activities in the organization. He completed his Bachelors from Dartmouth College, USA where he studied Physics and Neuroscience. Currently, he is doing research at Albert Einstein College of Medicine, USA. Along with neuroscience and physics, Laxman’s interest lies in learning Sanskrit, exploring ancient texts, reforming the education system, and hiking.

Education and Outreach Programs Team

Team Lead: Arati Sharma

Arati is a 4th year PhD student in Neuroscience at Dartmouth College. Broadly, she is interested in understanding how and why we make the decisions that we do. At ANPAN, she will work closely with the Education and outreach team to build education modules with the specific goal of elucidating how findings and insights from neuroscientific studies can be applied in our daily lives. In her free time Arati enjoys hiking, climbing trees, experimenting with cooking, and exploring libraries.

Content Development Team

Team Lead: Nischal Bhandari

Nischal Bhandari is a rising senior at Ramapo College of New Jersey and is pursuing Bioinformatics and Data Science. In his free time, he enjoys writing, watching movies, and walking around. At ANPAN, he works in content design and research with an aim to make topics in brain sciences more accessible to aspiring students and general readers.

Social Media Outreach Team

Team Lead: Garima Rokaya

Garima Rokaya is a junior honors psychology major at Howard University from Nepalgunj, Banke, Nepal. She is the leading social media coordinator at ANPAN. She loves networking with people and believes in chain of association, wondering how the slightest interaction of one person can create an impact in another person’s life. She loves learning about culture and connecting with people from different parts of the world. She is fascinated by intercultural psychology and wants to explore more about that aspect within psychology. She has been to Costa Rica, UK and France, and she is now manifesting to go to Africa. She believes in the law of attraction.

Web Development and Graphic Design Team

Team Lead: Praful Sigdel

Praful Sigdel is an earthling a.k.a “human being” who finds interest in looking at things beyond their definitions, their images, and their common perception that all “human being” are either conditioned or wired to perceive. On a personal journey to find answers to Life’s big questions: “Who am I? And “What is and Why Education?” Praful completed his formal academic degree with a Bachelors of Science in Physics from Fisk University and Bachelors of Engineering in Mechanical Engineering from Vanderbilt University. Praful’s informal educational interest lies in Evolutionary Biology, Genetics, Neuroscience, and Ornithology. Currently, Praful holds the responsibility to lead and perform Website Development and Graphic Design with ANPAN.
